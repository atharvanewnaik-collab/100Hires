# Tom Critchlow

## Overview

Tom Critchlow is an independent SEO consultant, growth strategist, and advisor known for bridging technical SEO with business strategy. Having previously worked at Distilled and later advising startups and enterprise organizations, he focuses on helping companies adapt their search strategies to emerging technologies, organizational change, and AI-driven search experiences. His work emphasizes strategic thinking over tactical optimization, making him a respected voice on the future of SEO.

## Why Selected

Tom Critchlow was selected for his strategic perspective on the evolution of search in the age of artificial intelligence. Rather than focusing solely on ranking techniques, his work explores how AI is fundamentally changing search behavior, user expectations, and the role of SEO within organizations. His insights help businesses rethink long-term SEO strategies for an AI-first search landscape.
