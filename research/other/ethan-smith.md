# Ethan Smith

## Overview

Ethan Smith is the Co-founder and CEO of Graphite, a growth agency specializing in SEO and AI-driven search strategies for leading technology companies. He is also an instructor at Reforge, where he teaches modern SEO and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). His work focuses on helping businesses adapt to AI-powered search by developing scalable content strategies, optimizing for large language models (LLMs), and improving brand visibility across emerging search platforms.

## Why Selected

Ethan Smith was selected for his pioneering work in AI Search Optimization and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO). His research and educational content provide practical frameworks for optimizing content in AI-driven search environments, making his insights highly valuable for understanding the future of AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS organizations.
