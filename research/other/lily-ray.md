# Lily Ray

## Overview

Lily Ray is the Vice President of SEO Strategy & Research at Amsive and one of the most respected voices in technical SEO, Google's E-E-A-T principles, and AI-powered search. Through her research, conference presentations, and industry publications, she helps businesses understand how search quality, trust signals, and AI-generated search experiences are reshaping SEO. Her work combines data-driven analysis with practical recommendations for maintaining long-term search visibility.

## Why Selected

Lily Ray was selected for her expertise in Google's quality systems and the evolution of AI-powered search. Her research explains how businesses can optimize content for both traditional search engines and AI-driven answer engines while maintaining strong E-E-A-T signals. Her work is particularly valuable for B2B SaaS organizations seeking sustainable organic growth in an AI-first search landscape.

## Notes

Lily Ray's work emphasizes that AI Search Optimization should complement—not replace—the core principles of high-quality SEO. Her research consistently highlights that trustworthy, expert-driven content and strong E-E-A-T signals remain essential for achieving visibility across both traditional search engines and AI-powered answer engines.
