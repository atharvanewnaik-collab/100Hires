# Kevin Indig

## Overview

Kevin Indig is an organic growth advisor, SEO strategist, and author of the Growth Memo newsletter. Having previously led SEO and growth initiatives at Shopify, Atlassian, and G2, he now advises some of the world's fastest-growing technology companies on organic growth, AI search visibility, and content strategy.
