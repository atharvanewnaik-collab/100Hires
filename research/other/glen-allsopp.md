# Glen Allsopp

## Overview

Glen Allsopp is an SEO researcher, entrepreneur, and founder of Detailed, a platform renowned for publishing data-driven SEO research and industry analyses. His work focuses on large-scale studies, search trends, AI-powered search behavior, and actionable SEO strategies for businesses. Through original research and experimentation, he provides evidence-based insights into how search engines and AI systems evaluate, rank, and surface content.

## Why Selected

Glen Allsopp was selected for his rigorous, research-oriented approach to SEO. Rather than relying on opinions or best practices alone, his work is grounded in large-scale datasets and empirical analysis. His studies on AI-powered search and evolving search ecosystems provide valuable insights for organizations adapting their content strategies to AI-driven search experiences.
