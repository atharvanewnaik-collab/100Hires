# Ross Simmonds

## Overview

Ross Simmonds is the Founder and CEO of Foundation Marketing, a B2B content marketing agency specializing in content strategy, SEO, and content distribution. He is also the author of *Create Once, Distribute Forever*, a widely recognized framework for maximizing the reach and impact of content. His work focuses on helping organizations create high-quality content, leverage AI responsibly, and build scalable distribution systems that drive sustainable organic growth.

## Why Selected

Ross Simmonds was selected for his expertise in content marketing, AI-assisted content creation, and strategic content distribution. While many SEO experts focus primarily on content production, Ross emphasizes that successful SEO also depends on effectively distributing, repurposing, and amplifying content. His insights are particularly valuable for B2B SaaS organizations seeking to maximize the return on AI-powered content production.
