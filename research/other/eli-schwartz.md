# Eli Schwartz

## Overview

Eli Schwartz is an independent SEO consultant, advisor, and author of *Product-Led SEO*. He previously led SEO at SurveyMonkey and has advised numerous SaaS and technology companies on scalable organic growth strategies. His recent work focuses on how AI-powered search is reshaping SEO and content strategy.

---

## Why Selected

Eli Schwartz was selected because he combines enterprise SEO experience with practical business strategy. His work explains how organizations can adapt SEO investments, measure AI Search impact, and align search initiatives with broader business objectives.
