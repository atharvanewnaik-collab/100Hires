# Eli Schwartz

## Overview

Eli Schwartz is an SEO strategist, consultant, and author of *Product-Led SEO*. He is widely recognized for developing strategic SEO frameworks that prioritize sustainable business growth over keyword rankings. His expertise spans product-led SEO, SaaS growth, international SEO, and the evolving role of AI in search. Through consulting, writing, and speaking engagements, he helps organizations build scalable SEO strategies that align with business objectives.

## Why Selected

Eli Schwartz was selected for his strategic perspective on SEO and his emphasis on creating long-term competitive advantages through content and product strategy. His work bridges traditional SEO with emerging AI-powered search, making his insights highly relevant for understanding the future of SEO content production in B2B SaaS.
