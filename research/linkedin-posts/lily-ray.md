# Lily Ray — LinkedIn Research

## Overview

Lily Ray is the Vice President of SEO and AI Search at Amsive and the founder of Algorhythm. She regularly publishes research and commentary on AI-powered search, Google's ranking systems, enterprise SEO, and content quality. Her LinkedIn posts combine technical expertise with practical observations from real-world SEO projects.
