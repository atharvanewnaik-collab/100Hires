# The Brave New World of SEO

## Overview

In his opening keynote at SEO Week 2025, Mike King argues that search has entered a fundamentally new era driven by AI-powered retrieval systems, conversational interfaces, and generative search experiences. He challenges SEO professionals to move beyond traditional optimization practices and introduces **Relevance Engineering**—a multidisciplinary framework designed for modern search environments.

The presentation explores how AI Overviews, vector embeddings, semantic retrieval, and evolving user behavior are reshaping search, while outlining the technical and strategic capabilities that marketers need to remain visible in AI-powered discovery systems.

## Summary

Mike King explains that search is rapidly evolving from a keyword-driven ranking system into an AI-powered information retrieval ecosystem. Technologies such as AI Overviews, vector embeddings, semantic retrieval, and conversational search are changing how search engines understand content and how users discover information.

Central to the presentation is Mike's concept of **Relevance Engineering**, which expands SEO beyond traditional ranking tactics. Rather than relying on checklists and isolated best practices, Relevance Engineering combines information retrieval, artificial intelligence, content strategy, user experience, digital PR, and technical SEO to improve visibility across multiple search surfaces.

He argues that many SEO tools and workflows have failed to keep pace with modern search systems, which increasingly rely on semantic understanding instead of simple keyword matching. As a result, marketers need to understand how AI retrieves, evaluates, and synthesizes information if they want their content to remain discoverable.

Mike also demonstrates practical AI-powered tooling, including an AI Overview simulator that helps evaluate how content may perform in Google's AI-generated answers. Throughout the keynote, he encourages SEO professionals to experiment, build their own solutions, and adopt a more technical, research-driven approach to optimization.

## Key Insights

- Search is increasingly powered by semantic retrieval, vector embeddings, and AI-generated responses rather than traditional keyword matching.
- AI Overviews and conversational search experiences are changing how users discover and consume information.
- **Relevance Engineering** is presented as a modern framework that integrates information retrieval, AI, content strategy, UX, digital PR, and technical SEO.
- Traditional SEO checklists and generic best practices are no longer sufficient for AI-powered search environments.
- Organizations should optimize content for meaning, topical depth, and relevance rather than focusing solely on keywords.
- AI-powered search requires marketers to understand how retrieval systems identify, rank, and synthesize information.
- Building custom workflows and open-source solutions can provide greater flexibility than relying exclusively on conventional SEO tools.
- Modern SEO success depends on creating content that AI systems can accurately retrieve, interpret, and reference across multiple search experiences.
