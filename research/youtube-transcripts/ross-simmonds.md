# Content Distribution in the Age of AI

## Overview

In this presentation, Ross Simmonds explains why content creation alone is no longer enough to drive organic growth in the AI era. As search evolves beyond Google into AI assistants, social platforms, communities, and generative search experiences, marketers must build systematic content distribution engines that maximize the reach and lifespan of every content asset.

Rather than viewing AI as a replacement for marketers, Ross demonstrates how AI can accelerate content repurposing, research, and distribution while emphasizing that strategy, creativity, and audience understanding remain human responsibilities.

## Summary

Ross Simmonds argues that modern content marketing has become overly focused on publishing while neglecting distribution. In an environment where AI makes content creation faster and cheaper, competitive advantage increasingly comes from ensuring valuable content reaches audiences across multiple channels.

He introduces a content growth framework built around **research, creation, distribution, and optimization**, emphasizing that distribution should be treated as a core marketing function rather than an afterthought. Instead of relying solely on search traffic, organizations should actively distribute content through platforms such as LinkedIn, Reddit, newsletters, podcasts, social media, and niche communities where their audiences already spend time.

The presentation also explores how AI can support marketers by automating repetitive tasks such as content repurposing, summarization, ideation, and workflow execution. However, Ross stresses that AI should enhance—not replace—human creativity, strategic thinking, and editorial judgment. The most successful teams will combine AI efficiency with authentic storytelling, audience research, and high-quality content.

For B2B SaaS companies, this approach creates a scalable content engine that extends the impact of every asset while improving brand visibility across both traditional search and emerging AI-powered discovery platforms.

## Key Insights

- Content creation is only one part of a successful content marketing strategy; distribution is equally important.
- AI reduces the cost of creating content, making effective distribution a stronger competitive advantage.
- Every content asset should be repurposed into multiple formats for different channels and audiences.
- A repeatable content growth framework consists of research, creation, distribution, and optimization.
- Audience research should determine where and how content is distributed rather than assuming search is the only discovery channel.
- AI can accelerate repurposing, research, summarization, and workflow automation while human marketers provide strategic direction and creativity.
- Modern marketers should optimize for visibility across social platforms, communities, newsletters, podcasts, and AI-powered search experiences—not just Google Search.
- Sustainable growth comes from building repeatable distribution systems instead of relying on one-time publishing efforts.
