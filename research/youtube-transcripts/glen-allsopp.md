# AI Apps, Reports & Workflows Every Marketer Should Steal

## Overview

In this Ahrefs Podcast episode, Glen Allsopp demonstrates how he uses custom AI applications and automation to streamline SEO research, content marketing, and repetitive marketing tasks. Rather than discussing AI conceptually, he shares practical workflows that marketers can build with modern AI tools to save time while improving the quality and consistency of their work.

The session focuses on applying AI to real marketing problems, including content research, competitor analysis, AI visibility reporting, and workflow automation. It highlights how marketers can create specialized AI-powered tools without extensive programming knowledge.

## Summary

Glen Allsopp explains that the greatest value of AI lies not in generating generic content but in automating repetitive marketing tasks and creating purpose-built applications that support strategic decision-making. Drawing from his own experiments, he demonstrates several custom AI workflows that significantly reduce manual effort while improving research quality.

The webinar showcases practical examples, including an AI-powered Reddit research assistant for identifying content opportunities, an automated competitor backlink analysis tool, AI-generated marketing reports, customized productivity applications, and brand-aware content generation workflows. Rather than relying on generic prompts, Glen advocates building reusable systems that integrate AI into day-to-day marketing operations.

A recurring theme is that AI should augment human expertise instead of replacing it. By combining proprietary data, structured workflows, and human judgment, marketers can use AI to increase efficiency while maintaining quality and strategic control. The discussion also emphasizes experimentation, encouraging teams to build lightweight internal tools tailored to their own processes instead of depending solely on off-the-shelf software.

---

## Key Insights

- AI delivers the greatest value when it automates repetitive workflows rather than replacing strategic thinking.
- Custom AI applications can be built for specific marketing tasks without requiring extensive software development experience.
- Community platforms such as Reddit can be analyzed with AI to uncover customer pain points and content opportunities at scale.
- AI can automate competitor research, backlink analysis, reporting, and other time-intensive SEO activities.
- Brand-specific context should be incorporated into AI workflows to generate more accurate and consistent marketing outputs.
- Modular AI workflows are more reliable and reusable than one-off prompts.
- Human expertise remains essential for interpreting results, making strategic decisions, and validating AI-generated outputs.
- Organizations should experiment with lightweight AI tools that integrate directly into existing marketing and SEO workflows rather than treating AI as a standalone solution.
